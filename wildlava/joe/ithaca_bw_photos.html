Joe's Photo Gallery


One of the most beautiful areas in which I've lived is the countryside near Lake Cayuga. I spent four excellent years in Ithaca, New York while attending Cornell University, and I often took time to explore the surrounding rolling hills and water. The following black and white pictures are the result of one such excursion with fellow photography enthusiast, Anita Krook...
