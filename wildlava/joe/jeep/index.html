My 1997 Jeep TJ

(Not shown are the 33" spare (which fits nicely and snugly after raising the center brake light) and the brand-new soft top. Also, it now has a new soft top - great to have clear windows again!)

So far, I've made the following customizations:

  • Rancho Rockcrawler 2.5" suspension lift
  • Rancho RS-9000 adjustable heavy-duty shocks
  • Heavier-duty steering stabilizer
  • BFG 33x9.50R15 Mud Terrain tires
  • Raised and modified center brake light to fit 33" spare (not shown)
  • Shorter axle gearing (changed from 3.07 to 3.73)
  • Brand-new soft top and upper door window skins (not shown)
  • Also have bikini top and deck cover for summer months
  • Newer improved factory exhaust manifold
  • New speedometer calibration gear
  • Minidisc player and power amp
  • Mopar hitch and bike rack
  • Removed that annoying "up-shift" indicator light
  • Removed the "Sport" decals
  • Removed the plastic bumper ends
